Cool Stuff

These are just cool things I like around the internet. Big or small, I just think they're neat.

  • TCRF - A wiki for unearthing unseen content in video games. A bit of "video game archeology". It's really cool to me and I've even contributed myself a few times. Perfect site to visit if I have a terrible connection and need something to read.
  • Wikipedia - I mean this one really needs no introduction, but the amount of time I spend just browsing around random interesting articles in my downtime warrants me putting it up here. I have contributed a few things before too, but not much. I get a kick out of seeing how some notable articles change over time :P
  • The Wayback Machine - Truly one of the most important archives of the modern age. This website is even in there! Though, you're not really missing much on that front.
  • BeepBox - A great chiptune online sequencer. I have made many game jam tunes using it!
